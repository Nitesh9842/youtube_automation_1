How to Download Instagram Reels

Simple 3-step process

1

Find the Reel on Instagram

Open Instagram and navigate to the Reel you want to download. Click the three dots menu and select "Copy Link".

Tip: Works with both public and accessible Reels
2

Paste the URL

Paste the copied link into the input field above. Make sure the URL is complete and valid.

Tip: URL should start with https://www.instagram.com/reel/
3

Click Download

Click the "Download Reel" button and wait a few seconds. Your video will be downloaded automatically.
